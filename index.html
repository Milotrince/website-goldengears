Who We Are
We are a nonprofit (12 U.S. Code § 3051) robotics team based in La Cañada, California. We aspire to not only succeed in the FIRST Tech Challenge, an international high school robotics competition, but also provide opportunities to the younger generations in STEM.
Our Impact
  • 15 students
  • 3 robots designed
  • 10 experts reached
  • 1000 young minds impacted
  • Mentoring FIRST Lego League teams
  • Mentoring fellow FIRST Tech Challenge teams
  • Assisting the local Science National Honor Society
  • Hosting summer workshops for elementary and middle school students
  • Providing equipment to low-income robotics teams
  • Aiding the Tournament of Roses in their float construction
